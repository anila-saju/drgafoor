AYURVEDA DEPARTMENT - Senior Physician

Dr. Shareefa Thesni obtained her bachelors Degree B.A.M.S (Bachelor in Ayurveda Medicine and Surgery) from Vaidyaratnam Ayurveda College, Ollur, Kerala in the Year 2004. Her firm belief that Nature always helps those who serve others have led her to serve the society through Ayurveda.


Dr. Shareefa Thesni Started her Ayurveda Practitioner career at a well known Ayurveda Hospital in kerala ( Keraleeya Ayurveda Samajam, Shornur ) and also has experience in the field of teaching as Associate Lecturer in Santhigiri Ayurveda Medical College, Palakkad.


Since 2011, She is working in Dr. Gafoor’s Ayurvedic Alternative Medical Center. She is skilled in performing classical Panchakarma and Kerala Specialised Therapeutic Procedures. She maintains a Holistic Approach in treatments which has brought marvellous results in her patients.
